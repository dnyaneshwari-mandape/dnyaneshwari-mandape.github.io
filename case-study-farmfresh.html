FarmFresh isn't a new app. It's Instamart's existing produce catalog with one layer added: who grew it, when, how it was checked, and what happens if it's wrong. Built from the FarmFresh Trust Experience design source — 14 componentized screens replacing an earlier 11-screen static pass.

We are not selling vegetables. We are making every purchase transparent.

03 Discovery

Nine insights. Each one is a screen decision, not an observation.

Badges don't survive scrutiny. A source, an inspector, and a fee line item do.

01
A claim needs a source, not a badge.
→ Every claim attributed
02
Trust needs one comparable number.
→ Freshness Score, 96/100
03
5 PDP accordions is where trust gets ignored.
→ 2 dedicated exit screens
04
Shoppers ask "how fresh now," not just "where."
→ Live freshness tracking
05
A delivery slot is a freshness promise too.
→ Slots paired to harvest time
06
"Supports farmers" needs a price, not ad copy.
→ Itemized Farmer Support Fee
07
Trust needs a no-fault failure mode.
→ Guarantee shown pre-payment
08
A crop's story should run forward, not backward.
→ Seed-to-door, chronological
09
No error states means engineering can't estimate it.
→ System states shipped as screens
04 Strategy

Trust is the differentiator. Checkout is where it's tested.

Speed and price get matched within a quarter. A verified farm, a named inspector, and a score don't — if they survive all the way to payment.

Trust-first every screen answers "how do you know" before asking for money Progressive disclosure headline signal instant, full audit one tap away Reuse Instamart nav, cart bar, chips — nothing new to learn but content Built to estimate component library + system states ship as screens

06 Validation

What gets tested before this ships

Nothing here has been tested. This is the plan, and the two riskiest bets.

HypothesisHow we'd measure itRisk if wrong
A computed score beats badges aloneTime-to-add, PDP→Trust click-throughUnexplained 96/100 reads as theater
A priced fee reads as trust, not taxA/B vs. an unnamed platform fee"Am I paying twice for one promise?"
Forward journey reads further than reverse trackingScroll depth, time-on-screenLow — worst case, reverts to reverse order

Next iteration: make the Freshness Score tappable to its formula breakdown directly on the Trust screen — the number needs to survive a skeptical tap, not just a caption.
